Planning UX Tests

Learn to define objectives, select participants, and structure scenarios that align with real-world use cases. This section provides a clear framework to create effective, goal-driven UX tests for actionable insights.

📄️ Defining Test Objectives

Clear objectives are the foundation of any successful UX test. Without them, you risk gathering data that’s interesting but ultimately unhelpful. Objectives focus your testing efforts, ensuring every task and question aligns with your goals.

📄️ Identifying Target Participants

The right participants can make or break your UX test. They’re the key to uncovering actionable insights that truly represent your audience. This section helps you pinpoint the people who matter most for your research and ensures your findings are both relevant and impactful.

📄️ Structuring Test Scenarios

A well-structured test scenario is the backbone of effective UX testing. It provides the context and tasks participants need to interact with your product naturally and deliver meaningful insights. This section walks you through crafting scenarios that mimic real-world usage while aligning with your objectives.

📄️ Writing Test Tasks

Test tasks are the heartbeat of a UX test—they direct participants' actions and reveal how effectively your product supports their goals. Well-written tasks provide clarity, focus, and actionable insights. This section will teach you how to craft tasks that guide participants while letting their natural behavior shine.
